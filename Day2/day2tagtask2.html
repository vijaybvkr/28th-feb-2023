Lepakshi

Lepakshi is a village in the anantapurSri Sathya Sai district of Andhra Pradesh, India.[2] It is located 15 km (9.3 mi) east of Hindupur and approximately 82 km (51 mi) west of Kadiri and 120 km (75 mi) north of Bangalore.Veerabhadra temple is a Hindu temple located in the Lepakshi, in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. The temple is dedicated to the Virabhadra, a fierce incarnation of Lord Shiva. Built in the 16th century, the architectural features of the temple are in the Vijayanagara style with profusion of carvings and paintings at almost every exposed surface of the temple.

Bending forward, he passed the twig slowly under the pillar. From one end to the other!
